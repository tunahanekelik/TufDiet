TufDiet Meal Timing and Frequency Guidelines

Optimal Meal Distribution:
- Breakfast (25% of daily calories): 7-9 AM, focus on protein and complex carbs.
- Lunch (35% of daily calories): 12-2 PM, largest meal with balanced macros.
- Dinner (30% of daily calories): 6-8 PM, lighter with emphasis on protein and vegetables.
- Snacks (10% of daily calories): optional, focus on protein and fiber.

Intermittent Fasting Approaches:
- 16:8 protocol: eat within an 8-hour window, fast for 16 hours.
- 14:10 protocol: gentler option, suitable for beginners.
- Best for weight maintenance and metabolic flexibility.

Pre-Workout Nutrition:
- Eat 2-3 hours before exercise for full meals.
- 30-60 minutes before: light snack with carbs and moderate protein.
- Avoid high-fat and high-fiber foods before workouts.

Post-Workout Nutrition:
- Consume protein within 30-60 minutes after exercise.
- Combine protein with carbohydrates for optimal recovery.
- Aim for 20-30g of protein post-workout.

Late Night Eating:
- Avoid heavy meals 2-3 hours before bedtime.
- If hungry before bed, choose protein-rich snacks.
- Late night eating can disrupt sleep quality and digestion.

Meal Prep Strategies:
- Prepare meals for 3-4 days in advance.
- Use portion control containers for accurate tracking.
- Batch cook proteins and grains for quick assembly.
